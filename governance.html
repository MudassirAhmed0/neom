About the Division

Governance, Risk and Compliance It is managing overall governance, risk management and compliance with regulations. Governance: Ensuring that NEOM activities support NEOM's business goals. Risk: The identification, classification and addressing of any risk associated with NEOM activities. Compliance: Ensuring that an NEOM is meeting compliance with all legal and regulatory requirements.
