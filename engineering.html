About the Division

Cybersecurity Engineering and Architecture is necessary to ensure cybersecurity and architectural principles and controls are embedded right from the early stage of system, software and architecture design and development for IT and ICS/OT environment.

Key Activities

CYBERSECURITY ENGINEERING
ARCHITECTURE PLANNING,
DESIGN AND MAINTENANCE
  • 1.Establish and maintain cybersecurity architecture planning, design, and maintenance service in order to specify the organizational structure, functional behavior of a computing environment that includes network, applications, and security features, as well as how these elements interact with each other.
  • 2.Define an enterprise-wide cybersecurity architecture framework, boundaries and defense in depth strategy
  • 3.Maintain a secure enterprise architecture through lesson learnt, external or internal factors (political, economic, social, technological, legal), change to threat landscape, change to business requirement, and cybersecurity assessment results
Full Cybersecurity Architecture Plan
  • 1.Identify and define the scope of technology and platform security at NEOM​
  • 2.Identify the in-scope elements, applications, technologies, platforms, etc. for ensuring security​
  • 3.Ensure security of the in-scope applications or infrastructures​
