Mission

We Keep Neom Cyber-Secure!

The role of the cybersecurity sector is to ensure NEOM is able to prevent and withstand cyber-attacks. We intend to enable resiliency within NEOM and make NEOM a secure digital utopia.

We perform and provide a number of services for sectors and departments within NEOM that include cyber risk assessments, compliance assessments, third party risk management, security configuration reviews, among many others. These services are intended to support the critical information infrastructure, sectors, departments and residents within NEOM. As part of our day-to-day activities, we also ensure an efficient and effective response to security incidents, if and when they occur.

NEOM will be the most secured humanity-centered cognitive region where bold dreams are realised. Mesfer Almesfer Cybersecurity Director (CISO)

As NEOM moves toward rapid digitalization and technological advancements, focusing on disruptive technologies to develop an advanced IT and system infrastructure, the potential risk of cyber-attacks increases with it. And to describe cybersecurity at NEOM, I would like to highlight an old axiom which may still be true: ”An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure”.

In order to ensure cyber risks are duly mitigated, NEOM needs to adopt a security-by-design approach that embeds risk thinking from the onset of all new business initiatives and enables trust at all layers of the cognitive landscape. The architects and engineers of NEOM need to exercise great care and more than a little wisdom. The mission of the architects of the key urban infrastructure of the future must do more than to create efficient and highly functional infra that is reliable, low in energy consumption and polluting effects, cost effective, easily repairable and updatable in terms of future improvements. Perhaps the top design criteria are to create smart control systems that are protected against cyber-attacks, able to detect intrusions, isolate infected control systems, and restore systems to normal operations with maximum efficiency.

While securing the systems through security controls is a key factor, the success of the NEOM cybersecurity program will also depend on its ability to build a security-aware culture that enables a human firewall. This is where we introduce the Cybersecurity Portal, which is envisioned to be a one-stop shop for all things cybersecurity. It will consist of specially curated content for residents, businesses, public and private sector organisations, leadership, etc. and will consist of newsletters, thought leaderships, description of cybersecurity services offered, policies, guidelines and a whole lot more.

In order to keep the content of the portal relevant and fine-tuned to the audience members, we would appreciate any feedback and you can write to us on cybersecurity@neom.com

Thank You!
